# Business-Data-Analysis
Comparing and analyzing business data set using both python and excel
Mini Project 
Business Problem Statement
Scenario
You have recently joined Amazon India as a Junior Data Analyst.
The Sales and Operations team has provided you with sales transaction data from different states, product categories, payment modes, and customers.
The management wants to understand the overall business performance before planning the next quarter's strategy.
As a Data Analyst, your responsibility is to:
•	Clean the dataset. 
•	Analyze sales and profit. 
•	Identify top-performing products and categories. 
•	Find underperforming regions and products. 
•	Create dashboards and visualizations. 
•	Present business recommendations. 
Your analysis should help management answer key business questions and make data-driven decisions.
